Setup Instructions

Recommended setup: connect the dedicated Google account that already manages your calendars.

  1. Open Settings in AthletiSync and enter the Google OAuth Client ID, Client Secret, and Redirect URI in the Google OAuth section.
  2. In the form below, enter a profile name such as District Calendar Manager.
  3. Click Start Google Sign-In.
  4. Sign in with the dedicated Google account that already has edit access to the calendars.
  5. Approve the requested Google Calendar access.
  6. After you return here, AthletiSync saves the connected Google account as an auth profile.
  7. Then use Add Calendar to save a separate calendar destination record.
  8. When you add the calendar destination, enter a label, the exact Google Calendar ID, and select the connected auth profile.
  9. Use the Test Calendar Access button in the calendar list to confirm that the connected account has writer or owner access to that specific calendar.

This saves the Google user account connection. It does not create a calendar destination by itself.
