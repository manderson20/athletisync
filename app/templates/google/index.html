Setup Instructions

Use this section to add the Google account AthletiSync should use for calendar access.

  1. Fill in the Google OAuth Configuration section on this page and save it.
  2. In the form below, enter a profile name such as District Calendar Manager.
  3. Click Sign In With Google.
  4. Sign in with the Google account that already has access to the calendar you want AthletiSync to manage.
  5. Approve the requested Google Calendar access.
  6. After Google sends you back to this page, AthletiSync saves that Google account as an auth profile.
  7. Repeat this section again if you want to connect a second Google account.
  8. Then use Add Calendar to save a separate calendar destination record.
  9. When you add the calendar destination, enter a label, the exact Google Calendar ID, and select the connected auth profile.
  10. Use the Test Calendar Access button in the calendar list to confirm that the connected account has writer or owner access to that specific calendar.

This is where you add the Google account AthletiSync will use. It saves the Google account connection only. You will add the calendar destination separately in the next section.
